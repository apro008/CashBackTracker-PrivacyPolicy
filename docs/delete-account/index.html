How to Delete Your Account In-App

You can delete your DailyFinance account and all associated data directly from within the app. No need to contact us — the deletion is instant and permanent.

  1. Open the DailyFinance app on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom-right icon).
  3. Scroll down to the About & Support section.
  4. Tap Delete Account & Data (shown in red).
  5. Confirm twice when prompted.
Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your transactions, budgets, savings goals, and financial data will be deleted immediately.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your Firebase authentication account (email, display name)
  • All local app data — transactions, categories, budgets, debts, savings goals, recurring transactions, bill reminders, group expenses, and all other financial records stored on your device

What Is Not Deleted

  • Google Drive backups — if you previously backed up your data to Google Drive, those files remain in your own Google Drive account. You can delete them manually from Google Drive.
